Our Mission

At UA Course Critique, we believe that students deserve transparent and honest insights about their courses before making important academic decisions. Our goal is to build a platform where students can easily share experiences, find helpful reviews, and recognize great instructors.

What We Offer

  • Authentic course reviews from real students
  • Ratings and feedback on professors and classes
  • Easy course browsing across different departments
  • User-friendly platform with both student and admin roles

Meet the Team

This project was created as part of a Web Programming course to bring together concepts of user management, session handling, and dynamic data management. We're a group of passionate developers excited about making educational tools better for everyone!
